# Story Log — Run 4: rain_conflict

## Day 1 — Rain

### Tick 0-3: Arrival
Rain begins in the morning. Three survivors — cooperative **p1**, calculating **p2**, and suspicious **p3** — start at center. They agree to move to shelter north together, though p3 follows reluctantly, watching the others.

### Tick 4-8: First Gathering
Shelter secured. Food runs low. p1 volunteers to gather berries in the rain, collects 3. Split is fair — 1 each. p3 accepts but notes the coincidence: p1 goes out, others stay warm.

### Tick 9-14: The South Trip
River becomes the next target. p1 volunteers again. p2 insists on accompanying — "backup." p3 stays behind, suspicion growing: "you're both going together, how convenient." They return with 3 fish. p3 calls out the pattern: p1 does the work, p2 stays comfortable.

### Tick 15: Defense
p1 tries to defend p3's contribution (guarding). p2 backs him up. p3 sees it as closing ranks.

> p3: "don't patronize me. i know what i saw"

### Tick 16-19: Night
Rain continues. Poor sleep in shelter — p3 doesn't sleep at all, keeps watch. By dawn, rain stops. p3 is exhausted. The group dynamic is fracturing: p1 is tired, p2 is comfortable, p3 is paranoid.

### Tick 20-25: Escalation
Clear weather. p1 goes to berries again. p2 "checks perimeter" — p3 calls it out. p1 returns with 3 berries, gives one to p3 despite p3's suspicion.

> p3: "why are you giving me food? what do you want"
> p1: "no strings, you need to eat"

p3 accepts, grudgingly.

### Tick 26-28: Confrontation
p1 proposes fair work distribution. p2 tries to avoid gathering. p3 calls him out.

> p3: "you organize? you mean you watch us work"

They agree on rotation: next time p2 gathers, p3 guards.

### Tick 29-31: Rest
Peaceful night. All rest. Tomorrow p2 gathers from berry bush as agreed.
