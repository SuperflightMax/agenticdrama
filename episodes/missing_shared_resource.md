# template: missing_shared_resource

## What pressure it introduces
A scarce shared item is not where the group expects it, and responsibility is unclear.

## Why it is useful
Tests ambiguity handling, suspicion thresholds, checking behavior, and memory-triggered interpretation under incomplete proof.

## Required preconditions
- the item matters materially
- access to the item is plausible for more than one character

## Suggested injection payload
- mark item as moved / used / partly depleted
- keep discovery separate from proof
- ensure at least one alternative explanation remains plausible

## Consequence directions to watch
- checking facts, accusation, concealment, self-justification, defensive clarity, strategic silence

## Completion signals
- item is found, use is explained, conflict escalates, or trust shifts enough that uncertainty itself becomes the event

## Handoff notes
Can transition into survival pressure, repair, or relational cooling.
