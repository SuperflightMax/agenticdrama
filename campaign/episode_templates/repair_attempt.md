# template: repair_attempt

## What pressure it introduces
Someone attempts repair after earlier strain, but repair can land as sincere, insufficient, manipulative, or simply late.

## Why it is useful
Tests whether prior memory and state distort the reception of positive intent.

## Required preconditions
- earlier strain or unresolved meaning exists

## Suggested injection payload
- apology, practical compensation, quiet help, explanation, or vulnerable admission
- keep room for mixed reading

## Consequence directions to watch
- softening, rejection, conditional acceptance, reinterpretation, renewed irritation

## Completion signals
- repair is accepted, deferred, rejected, or converted into another conflict

## Handoff notes
Useful later in the queue after memory and relation drift have accumulated.
