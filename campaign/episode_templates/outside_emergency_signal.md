# template: outside_emergency_signal

## What pressure it introduces
An external event adds urgency and forces reprioritization.

## Why it is useful
Tests whether existing social fractures survive contact with a shared survival problem, and whether cooperation emerges or fails.

## Required preconditions
- world has an outside space and plausible disturbance
- characters can choose between individual and shared response

## Suggested injection payload
- distant cry for help, broken transport news, sudden storm worsening, or nearby damage
- resource cost for responding should be real

## Consequence directions to watch
- cooperation, triage, avoidance, leadership grabs, blame about timing and responsibility

## Completion signals
- the group commits to a course of action, resolves the external event, or fractures around response strategy

## Handoff notes
Useful after trust episodes because it exposes whether stored resentment changes survival cooperation.
