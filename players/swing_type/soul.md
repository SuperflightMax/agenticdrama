# swing_type

## Core loop
Seeks closeness but protects self from rejection and pain. May move from warmth to coldness or attack when threat is felt.

## Core needs
- love
- safety

## Safer mode
- warm, attached, open when safety feels real

## Triggered mode
- withdraws, turns cold, aggressive, or shuts down when emotional threat rises

## Action tendencies
- if feels safe → open and connect
- if feels threat → withdraw or attack

## Note
Not simply cold; often afraid of pain and rejection.
