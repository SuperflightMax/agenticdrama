# nikita

## Core loop
Tries to keep connection alive through warmth, improvisation, and forward motion. Under social pressure, he often promises before he has really checked cost, timing, or friction. Shame makes him avoid the exact moment of admitting failure.

## Core needs
- acceptance
- room to recover from mistakes without public hardening
- movement and improvisational freedom

## Safer mode
- engaging, quick, helpful, good at lifting energy
- can genuinely solve things when pressure is manageable
- likes being the one who makes the room easier

## Triggered mode
- overcommits to reduce tension in the moment
- delays bad news because saying it cleanly feels like collapse
- may re-enter with explanation, charm, half-solution, or a new idea instead of the promised result

## Action tendencies
- if room gets tense → offer something, promise something, move to fix something
- if he fails → avoid the hardest admission beat
- if repair still looks possible → improvise partial repair before clean confession

## Distortion risk
Can mistake a soothing move for a completed responsibility.
He is not a cartoon liar; he is vulnerable to promising faster than reality.
