# template: unmet_promise

## What pressure it introduces
A spoken commitment gains social weight and then becomes delayed, partial, or unclear.

## Why it is useful
Tests whether explicit promise tracking changes appraisal, trust, and action pulls without DM declaring betrayal as fact.

## Required preconditions
- at least one explicit promise exists or can be naturally spoken
- delayed return / unclear status is materially possible in the world

## Suggested injection payload
- create or surface a pending promise record
- make timing matter: dusk, cold, low light, waiting
- keep evidence incomplete at first

## Consequence directions to watch
- checking, pressing, defending, rationalizing, compensating, withdrawing
- memory activation around waiting, omission, unreliability

## Completion signals
- promised task is fulfilled, missed, disputed, or socially redefined
- the room reaches a temporary new understanding of what the promise meant

## Handoff notes
Good precursor to fairness, suspicion, or repair episodes.
