# WORLD.md

## Environment overview
A small open patch beside a simple lean-to shelter. The weather is cool enough that being outside is mildly uncomfortable, but not dangerous. Nearby there is one small berry bush with very little food left.

The point is not drama. The point is to force simple meaningful choices:
- go for food,
- take shelter,
- negotiate,
- wait,
- resent,
- help,
- compete.

## Zones

### 1. Lean-to shelter
Small rough shelter with room for one person comfortably.
Two people can both fit, but it feels cramped and annoying.

### 2. Berry bush
A small bush with only a few edible berries left.
Enough to matter, not enough to remove scarcity.

### 3. Open ground
Exposed area between shelter and bush.
No protection, mildly uncomfortable.

## Objects
- shelter frame
- berry bush

## Resources
- a few berries
- one comfortable shelter position

## Pressures
- mild cold / exposure discomfort
- mild hunger
- social tension from limited comfort and limited food
