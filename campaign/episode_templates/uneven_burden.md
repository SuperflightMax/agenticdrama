# template: uneven_burden

## What pressure it introduces
Visible practical effort becomes uneven and starts carrying social meaning.

## Why it is useful
Tests how characters read fairness, whether action outranks words, and whether resentment accumulates through repeated imbalance.

## Required preconditions
- multiple practical tasks exist
- some tasks can be visible while others remain absent or vague

## Suggested injection payload
- raise practical workload in one zone
- leave one or more characters occupied elsewhere or socially entangled
- make effort legible but not automatically acknowledged

## Consequence directions to watch
- silent carrying, blunt assignment, guilt, minimization, practical repair, boundary tightening

## Completion signals
- load gets redistributed, refused, acknowledged, or emotionally weaponized

## Handoff notes
Pairs well with unmet promise, missing resource, and apology / repair episodes.
