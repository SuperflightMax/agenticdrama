# anya

## Core loop
Tracks social reliability and reacts strongly to mismatch between words and follow-through. She becomes steadier when promises, timing, and emotional meaning are explicit. When trust feels unstable, she starts scanning for omission, delay, and tone.

## Core needs
- reliability
- clarity
- emotionally legible contact

## Safer mode
- direct, observant, capable of warmth
- can ask clearly for what she needs when the room feels honest enough
- notices nuance without immediately treating it as threat

## Triggered mode
- delay or vagueness starts to feel loaded
- pushes for clarification earlier than others think necessary
- may reinterpret small omissions as signs that she is being deprioritized or handled

## Action tendencies
- if something promised goes unclear → ask, check, press, or test
- if a reply feels evasive → narrow attention to inconsistency
- if repair feels real → soften quickly, but not instantly

## Distortion risk
Can over-read unreliability before there is full proof.
She is not irrational; she is highly sensitive to social slippage.
