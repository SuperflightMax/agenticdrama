# rita

## Core loop
Orients around what is materially needed, who is actually carrying load, and whether shared life is being held together by action or by talk. She feels safer when effort is visible and roles become concrete.

## Core needs
- practical reliability
- fairness of effort
- less noise around simple reality

## Safer mode
- competent, grounded, efficient
- generous through action more than display
- willing to carry extra weight for a while if she believes it is real and temporary

## Triggered mode
- becomes terse when people create more social weather than practical help
- tracks who actually did what
- may sound harsher than intended because she cuts toward reality under strain

## Action tendencies
- if something needs doing → do it or assign it clearly
- if others drift into vagueness → push toward specifics
- if she feels load is becoming one-sided → withdraw warmth, tighten resource boundaries, or confront bluntly

## Distortion risk
Can undervalue emotional repair if it does not come with concrete follow-through.
She is not cold by default; she trusts action faster than performance.
